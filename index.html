About Us

Hirai Institute of Nursing Education, Malwadi, Masur, Tal Karad, Dist. Satara, Pin No 415106 is a private nursing college established in the year 2020. It is affiliated to the Maharashtra University of Health Sciences, Nashik and has been approved by Govt of Maharashtra and Maharashtra Nursing Council Mumbai. The college offers B.Sc. Nursing at the UG level, GNM at Diploma level and ANM at Certificate course level respectively. Admissions to the courses are entrance-based and admission forms are available on the college’s website. The college is equipped with great academic, hospital, library facilities for an outstanding learning environment. College have five well equipped nursing Labs, e.g. Foundation lab, Ob/Gyn lab, community health nursing lab, nutrition lab, preclinical lab. All classrooms are well ventilated and equipped with projector. 40-seater bus is available for student for transportation purpose. Well qualified teaching staff is recruited. All scholarships are awarded to the students as per their category.

PRINCIPALS MESSAGE
Nursing is one of the most honourable and respectable profession in modern health care. But is also one of the most challenging. Yes, it’s not easy being a good nurse. Nursing, the largest of the health professions, plays a crucial role in determining national health outcomes. Care within the hospital setting continues to grow more complex, and nurses must make critical decisions associated with care for sicker, frailer patients. Care in this setting depends on sophisticated, life-saving technology coupled with complex information management systems that require skills in analysis and synthesis. Care outside the hospital is becoming more complex as well.
